For educational purposes only
  • Use arrow keys and press/hold space to shoot lasers at the enemies!
  • Enemies can drop green upgrade spheres at a 4% droprate.
  • Collect the green upgrade spheres to empower your laser.
  • You will lose 10 HP if you get hit by an enemy laser.
  • The final upgrade reduces your laser cooldown. Fastest CD is 4.
  • I highly recommend playing with sound.
  • Press M or click on the sound icon to MUTE/UNMUTE
  • Press P to PLAY/PAUSE
  • Press R to RESET
